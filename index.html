About Me

I’m a Gameplay Programmer with experience in Unity and C#. I’ve published a game and continue to build new projects that challenge my creativity and problem-solving. Recently, I discovered a passion for shaders and graphics programming, and I’m aiming to grow into a more advanced technical role in game development. I enjoy crafting engaging game mechanics and designing experiences that feel fun and intuitive for players. While I often work solo, I’ve also collaborated with a small team to bring a project to life, which taught me the value of communication and coordination in game development. My technical toolkit is diverse: I’m learning HLSL to dive deeper into graphics programming, and I’m familiar with version control, Unreal Engine, and blueprint scripting. I also have experience with creative tools like Photoshop, Maya, and Blender, which helps me bridge the gap between art and code. Currently, my focus is on exploring advanced graphics programming and shaders, combining my love for gameplay systems with visually striking effects to create polished, immersive experiences. I’m always seeking new challenges that push both my coding skills and my creative thinking.

Spectral Maze

AI Chase Horror Prototype (Team Project)

A first-person horror game inspired by Dark Deception, created in a 3-member team. The core loop revolves around collecting and depositing tokens while avoiding a haunting enemy. The ghost AI dynamically spawns, despawns, and chases the player to keep tension high throughout the maze. My Role: Gameplay Programmer — implemented enemy AI, collectible limits and delivery system, player mechanics, and core game rules.

Slide & Dodge

2D Mobile Obstacle Dodger

A minimalist 2D mobile game where players dodge dynamically falling obstacles by moving horizontally. Obstacles spawn from the top with varying rotations, changing their falling direction and adding unpredictable challenge. I developed the game entirely myself, focusing on smooth controls, responsive feel, and polished mechanics.
